# XLSMART Package Advisor
### Find the right business connectivity package in 3 questions.

---

## The Problem

Indonesian SME owners face a 26+ product catalog across five categories — mobile, IoT, ICT, cloud, and AI — with no self-serve way to identify which solution fits their business. The result: leads drop off before they reach sales, and sales teams spend time on qualification that a tool should handle.

---

## The Solution

- An AI-powered advisor that maps an SME owner's profile to the right XLSMART package in under 60 seconds.
- Three inputs: industry (22 groups), company size (4 tiers), and primary connectivity need (8 options).
- One output: a specific product recommendation with a plain-language explanation and a direct call to action.

---

## How It Works

1. **Answer 3 questions** — industry, team size, and what you need most (connectivity, mobility, IoT, security, or collaboration).
2. **Get a personalized match** — the AI evaluates your inputs against the full XLSMART for BUSINESS catalog.
3. **Connect with sales** — one click opens the path to a consultation, no back-and-forth required.

---

## Why XLSMART

XLSMART for BUSINESS covers every connectivity layer an Indonesian SME or enterprise could need — from XL Satu Biz fiber for small shops to Internet Corporate SLA-guaranteed dedicated lines for multi-branch enterprises. With 26 products serving 330+ industries, the catalog depth is the asset. The advisor makes it accessible.
